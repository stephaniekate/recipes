Description: This drink is a wonderful aromatic bitters!

An easy-to-make tea with strong terpene notes. General health benefits, not recommended during pregnancy.

  1. Acquire
    • conifer needle sprouts
      • light-green new growth at branch tip
      • new conifer growth also edible as a small snack
      • positively identify tree as pine, spruce or fir before harvest
    • raw unfiltered honey, Grade B
    • proportions relative to output
  2. Collect conifer sprouts and place in mug
  3. Muddle sprouts to release oils
  4. Boil water and pour directly onto muddled sprouts
  5. Cover mug and allow to steep until temp cools to ~120ÂºF
  6. Remove sprouts that would impair the drinking experience with spoon
  7. Add honey and dissolve completely, stir to mix
  8. Enjoy as is, rich in vitamin-C
  9. Can also be served at hotter temperatures
    • nutrients found in honey degrade with heat
