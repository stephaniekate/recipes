Description: This drink is an effervescent and spicy treat!

An easy-to-make soda that is refreshing on a hot day and restorative on a cold day. Great all year long.

  1. Acquire
    • unwashed organic ginger
      • organic will have natural yeast and low toxicity
    • unbleached granulated sugar
    • filtered or distilled water
    • proportions relative to output
  2. Use glass jar containers with lids
  3. Boil water and allow to cool to ~120ÂºF
  4. Add sugar and dissolve completely, stir to mix
  5. Chop unwashed ginger into course chunks
  6. Add directly to sugar solution
  7. Cover loosely with open-weave cloth
  8. Let rest out of sunlight in a warm space
  9. Stir-in sugar and chopped ginger daily
  10. Repeat for one week until ready
  11. Ready when bubbles form, tastes like beer
  12. Mix with juice or iced tea in equal parts or enjoy as is
  13. Cover remaining ginger 'bug' loosely with lid
  14. Store in cool place: refrigerate
