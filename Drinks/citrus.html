sima for someone special

photo from The Adamant Kitchen

  1. Acquire
  2. organic unwashed citrus mix and match
    • lime, orange, lemon, grapefruit
      • organic will have natural yeast and low toxicity
  3. raw unfiltered honey
    • note: Grade B honey is higer quality than Grade A
  4. filtered or distilled water
  5. proportions relative to output
  6. Use glass jar containers with lids
  7. Boil water and allow to cool to ~120ÂºF
  8. Add honey and dissolve completely, stir to mix
  9. Slice unwashed citrus fruits in half
  10. Squeeze juice into honey solution
  11. Course chop rind and add to honey/citrus solution
  12. Cover loosely with open-weave cloth
  13. Rest out of sunlight in a warm space
  14. Stir-in honey and chopped ginger daily
  15. Repeat for one week until ready
  16. Ready when bubbles form
      17. raisins can be added with the rind; ready when they float
  17. Enjoy as is, tastes like sparkling lemonade
  18. Cover remaining sima loosely with lid
  19. Store in cool place aka refrigerate
